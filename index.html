Battleship Guide

Place ships

Ship nodes must be placed in neighboring squares going horizontally or vertically, but not diagonally. Ships may not overlap

Game

During the game, check the placement tab to veiw your ship locations and mark any hit ship by left clicking.

On the 2cd screen, you can mark hits and misses for your shots. Left click to indicate a hit and right click to indicate a miss. To remove an incorrect marking, click the square again.
